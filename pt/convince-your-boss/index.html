Investimento Inteligente

Why It's a Investimento Inteligente - The Numeros Speak for Themselves

Step-by-Step ROI Breakdown

  1. Calculate Your Investment: The initial sponsorship cost or delegate registration fee will be your investment. Example: If your sponsorship cost is $20,000, then $20,000 is the investment you'll be presenting.
  2. Estimate the Leads Generated: Based on the scale of the event and your company's objectives, estimate how many quality leads or connections you expect to generate at the conference. Example: If you expect to meet 50 qualified leads from operators, regulators, asset owners, and industry leaders, this will be your lead generation figure.
  3. Estimate the Conversion Rate: From your estimated leads, predict how many you can realistically convert into business opportunities. For instance, if you expect a 10% conversion rate from the 50 leads, this would mean 5 leads converting into opportunities.
  4. Estimate the Potential Value of Each Lead: Estimate the average revenue per lead based on your business model or past experience. Example: If the average deal size you close from each lead is $50,000, then the potential revenue from those 5 leads would be 5 x $50,000 = $250,000.
  5. Calculate Your ROI: To calculate your ROI, use the formula: ROI = Revenue from Leads / Investment x 100. Based on the numbers above: ROI = 250,000 / 20,000 x 100 = 1250% ROI. This means that for every $1 invested, the potential return is $12.50.
